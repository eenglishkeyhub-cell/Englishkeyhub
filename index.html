Overview

The Englishkeyhubis an English enrichment initiative for Grade 6 and Grade 7 girls. It helps students build confidence in reading, writing, speaking and creative expression.

Through projects, assemblies, competitions and weekly practice, students use English in real-life situations and discover that learning can be fun, meaningful, and full of new opportunities.
